Transform Molecule Coordinates

Transform Molecule Coordinates applies a rotation and translation to a molecule model or marker set.

There are several ways to start Transform Molecule Coordinates, a tool in the Movement category.

The Molecule should be chosen from the list of open molecule models. Its transformation is specified with:

  • Euler angles [φ θ ψ] - rotations defined by the χ-convention, where the first rotation is by an angle φ about the Z-axis, the second is by an angle θ (potentially ranging from 0 to π) about the new X-axis, and the third is by an angle ψ about the new Z-axis.

  • Shift [X Y Z] - translations along X, Y, and Z
Clicking Apply changes the atomic coordinates of the model (not its transformation matrix). The cumulative change from the original coordinates is reported in the dialog. Reset restores the original coordinates. Set is equivalent to clicking Reset and then Apply.

Note the original coordinates and cumulative change information will not be retained in a saved session.
