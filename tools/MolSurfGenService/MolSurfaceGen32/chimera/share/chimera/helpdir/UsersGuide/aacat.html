Amino Acid Categories

Amino acid categories appear in the Select menu and can be used in command-line specification.

The default amino acid categories (the default schemata in ResProp) are taken from:

"Prediction of protein secondary structure and active sites using the alignment of homologous sequences," M.J. Zvelebil, G.J. Barton, W.R. Taylor, and M. J. Sternberg, J. Mol. Biol., 195, 957 (1987).
